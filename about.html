PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY

    Makinley Butterfield was raised in Elk Ridge, Utah, where she trained at Lifehouse Performing Arts Academy. She continued to train as a member of Brigham Young University's Contemporary Dance Theatre while receiving her BFA in Dance, being seen as the student representative of this degree by her university. As a student, she performed works by such choreographers as Laja Field of LAJAMARTIN, Omar Roman de Jesus, Jesse Obremski, Jie-Hung Connie Shiau, Cue Arnold, and Cameron McKinney.
