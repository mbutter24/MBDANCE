About Me

I am Makinley Butterfield, a dancer, artist, and performer professionally trained in a variety of styles, including contemporary, modern, improvisation, ballet, jazz, tap, theater and street styles. With years of experience on stage and in the studio, I find joy in the creativity and dedication of every rehearsal process. In addition to performing, I have a background in teaching, sharing my love for dance with others, and choreographing, crafting movement that inspires and connects.

Lets connect!

My Vision

I aspire to build a career rooted in contemporary performance, as well as explore creative opportunities in theater, commercial work, film, modeling, and more. I want to continually push artistic boundaries and research within the process.
